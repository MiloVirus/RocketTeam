Story

Team Rocket was created as a Discord community by friends and for friends, it all started with the release of the game Call of Duty: Warzone. The initial in-game meta and map layouts provided campers with a huge advantage over aggressive style players but Team Rocket came with a simple but effective solution, Rocket Launchers.
